Introduction to ATS

An Applicant Tracking System (ATS) is a software application used by companies to streamline the recruitment process. Its primary function is to manage, filter, and rank job applications based on their relevance to a given job description. ATS scans resumes to identify keywords, qualifications, and skills that match the employer's requirements, helping recruiters efficiently shortlist candidates.

With the increasing volume of job applications for every vacancy, ATS has become a critical tool for hiring managers. It automates the initial screening process, saving time and ensuring that only the most qualified candidates are considered. However, for job seekers, passing through an ATS means optimizing their resumes with appropriate keywords and formatting to ensure compatibility.

An ATS plays a vital role in modern recruitment, bridging the gap between candidates and employers by making the hiring process more efficient, transparent, and organized.
